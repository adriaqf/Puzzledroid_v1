Why can't I play multiplayer?

Multiplayer mode is in development. Access Google to keep you informed of its development!

What do the colors of the levels mean?

The colour green corresponds to easy play mode, orange to normal play mode and red to hard play mode.

Why does a stopwatch appear when I play?

The stopwatch will put you on the ranking list. Challenge your friends to see who is fastest at solving the puzzles!

What are the colours of the classification?

They correspond to the difficulty of the puzzle carried out on the date indicated and with the score obtained.
